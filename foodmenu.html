Food Menu

The food at The Bulls Head is a rare find these days we serve traditional English meals made by us on site.

All the old classics like steak and stout pie, freshly battered fish and chips with mushy peas, great quality steaks along with a great choice of homemade specials changed daily. We have an unrivalled choice of up to fifteen desserts available everyday.

The pub is open everyday for food between 12pm and 2pm and every evening between 6.30pm and 9pm, and on Sundays we serve until 2.30pm.

Sunday lunch is served with a choice of four home-cooked joints, fresh vegetables, roast potatoes, stuffing, Yorkshire puddings and proper gravy made with meat stock. Monday through to Saturday we have a lunchtime special which includes a choice of any sweet in the price of £5.95
