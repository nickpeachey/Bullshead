For people who are travelling for pleasure you have an incredible amount of scenic and historic attractions to choose from. In the heart of the National Forest we have miles and miles of countryside walks quite literally on the doorstep.

The pub is very well located to visit Burton-upon-Trent, Derby, Leicester and Nottingham. The M42 is literally minutes away from which you can easily access the M1. In the opposite direction is the A38, and A444. East Midlands airport is only 15 miles away.

The following are also within easy each, Calke Abbey, Ashby castle, Conkers , Twycross Zoo, Drayton Manor, Moira furnace, Rosliston forestry centre, Staunton Harold resevoir, Foremark resevoir, Melbourne Hall, The Bass Museum of Brewing, Donington Park Racecourse, Repton school and many more...

We can offer overnight accomodation and also long-term stays with packages to include food if required. All double rooms are en-suite with flat-screen TV's, beverage facilities and wireless broadband internet access. Accomodation prices include a choice of continental or full English breakfast.
